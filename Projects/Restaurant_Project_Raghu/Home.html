About:-

Green Palace Restaurant Situated in the heart of Bangalore. A confluence of legendary Indian heritage & meticulous local craftsmanship, the restaurant boast of Indian, Oriental, Tandoori, Coastal and Nellore (Andhra) Cuisine. Treat friends and family to a memorable dining experience.Expect a journey of exciting flavours and textures showcasing the best of produce.We aim to convey our passion for cuisine, through a quest for perfect ingredients and technique, plus a deep-seated desire to continue learning how to evolve and blend old traditions and modern tastes.
